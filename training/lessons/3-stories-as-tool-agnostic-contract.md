# Lesson 3 — Stories as tool-agnostic contract

> Placeholder. Real lesson content lands in Epic 6.

A story file is the contract. Whether two engineers use Claude, Copilot, Codex, or OpenCode, if they agree on the story file the tool difference stops mattering. This is the lesson that makes BMAD adoption viable for mixed-tool teams.
