# Async cross-team story review lab

> Placeholder. Real lab content lands in Epic 6.

This is the async cross-team lab. A trainee on team A writes a story; a trainee on team B reviews it without ever pairing live; both teams meet briefly to compare notes on whether the story-as-contract held up. Plan ~30 minutes of writing + 30 minutes of asynchronous review + a 15-minute debrief.
